About Us

LLC "Well Energy Group" is a registered mandate company based in Moscow, serving as an authorized intermediary for established suppliers of petroleum products. We facilitate global transactions with integrity, efficiency, and secured returns for all intermediaries.

Our partners operate bonded storage terminals with ready access to high-demand oil and gas products, ensuring timely delivery and competitive pricing on CIF and FOB terms.

Available Petroleum Products

  • PETCOKE: Industrial-grade fuel for cement and steel production.
  • ULSD (EN590): Ultra-low sulfur diesel compliant with international standards.
  • JET A1: Aviation turbine fuel for commercial and cargo airlines.
  • LPG & LNG: Liquefied Petroleum and Natural Gas for energy distribution.
  • ESPO Crude: Eastern Siberia-Pacific Ocean grade crude oil.

Transaction Process

We maintain a simple and secure procedure that ensures full transparency, document verification, and guaranteed intermediary commissions at every stage.
  • Products available for immediate lift at storage or shipped via CIF/FOB.
  • Buyer issues LOI and company profile.
  • FCO and commercial invoice issued.
  • Final agreements signed after verification.
